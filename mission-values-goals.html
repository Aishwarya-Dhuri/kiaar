Mission, Core Values & Goals

Mission

To be a leading Interdisciplinary Research and Technology Development Institute of global importance that provides services to farmers and generates new knowledge for science, society and industry.

Core Values

The core values adopted by the Institute as enduring principles are

  • Innovative, need based and multi-disciplinary research
  • Conservation of Natural Resources
  • Smart Farming
  • Regenerative Agriculture
  • Customised Farm Advisory Services

Goals

  • To enhance productivity and improve the quality of agricultural produce.
  • To provide customised technical advisory services and quality inputs to farmers
  • To increase farmers income and protect the environment
  • To forge alliance with reputed national and international institutions
