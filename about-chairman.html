Godavari Biorefineries Limited (GBL)

This is a Bio refining company with Rs. 1400 crores ($200 million) in sales somaiya.com. The Company is engaged in the manufacture of sugar, ethanol, renewable energy, chemicals, biogas & compost from agricultural resources. The company also works extensively with 20,000 small farmers over 200 villages to improve productivity, yields & standard of living. The company’s products find use in food, fuels, solvents, cosmetics, fragrances, pharmaceuticals, mining, agriculture and variety of different applications. The products are of high quality and global demand.

Somaiya Trust and Somaiya Vidya Vihar (SVV) University

This is a trust involved in education, healthcare & rural development somaiya.com in the states of Maharashtra, Karnataka & Gujarat. The trust manages over 30 different educational institutions of learning extending from elementary to Ph.D. The education is imparted in diverse areas such as medicine, engineering, the arts and sciences, religion, vocational studies, education, languages etc. There are over 39,000 students and 1,500 faculty. There is also an urban teaching hospital with 650 beds & rural hospital with 40 beds. The trusts jointly run eight schools, six of which are in rural Maharashtra and Karnataka.
